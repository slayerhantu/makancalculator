Welcome to Makan Calculator

by Andy

We’ve all been there. You have a great meal with friends, the laughter is flowing, and then... the bill arrives. Suddenly, everyone pulls out calculators, math gets confusing, and someone always gets forgotten.

Makan Calculator fixes that. This app introduces a fair and stress-free bill splitting solution that handles service charges, calculates exact individual shares, and eliminates group chat awkwardness completely.
